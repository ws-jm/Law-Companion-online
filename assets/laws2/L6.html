CHAPTER L6 LAND USE ACT (VALIDATION OF CERTAIN LAWS, ETC.) ACT An Act to regularise the issuance of regulations by State Governments pursuant to the Land Use Act. [No. 94 of 1979.] [Date of commencement: 28th September, 1979] 1. Validation of certain laws, etc. (1) Notwithstanding anything to the contrary in the Land Use Act or any other enactment, all laws, and subsidiary legislation made at any time between the commencement of the Land Use Act and 30 September, 1979 by a Military Administrator (or former Military Governor), the Executive Council, a Commissioner or any other authority or any public officer of a State shall be deemed to have been validly made and shall have effect as if they had been made under or pursuant to the Land Use Act and accordingly, shall hereafter continue to have effect according to their tenor and intendment as if they were regulations made under or pursuant to section 46 of that Act.
