CHAPTER U3 UNIVERSITY OF BENIN TEACHING HOSPITAL MANAGEMENT BOARD ACT An Act to establish the University of Benin Teaching Hospital and a Management Board for the Teaching Hospital. [No. 51 of 1977.] [Date of commencement: 1st April, 1976] 1. Establishment of the Teaching Hospital and Management Board (1) There is hereby established a teaching hospital by the name of the University of Benin Teaching Hospital for the purpose of providing such facilities for the training of medical and other students as are usually provided by teaching hospitals of internationally high repute. (2) There shall be established for the management of the hospital a body to be known as the Uni-versity of Benin Teaching Hospital Management Board (in this Act referred to as 'the Board”) which shall be a body corporate with perpetual succession and a common seal. 2. Composition, functions etc., of the Board The composition, functions and powers of the Board established under section 1 (2) shall be as pro-vided for in the University Teaching Hospitals (Reconstitution of Boards, etc.) Act. [Cap. U15.] 3. Short title This Act may be cited as the University of Benin Teaching Hospital Management Board Act.
